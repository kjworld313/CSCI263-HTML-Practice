Lemon Poppy Seed Cake

Ingredients:
Unsalted Butter - Softened
Sugar
Yogurt - Room Temperature
Milk - Room Temperature
Lemon Zest
Lemon Extract
Vanilla Extract
Poppy Seeds
Eggs - Room Temperature
Baking power
Salt

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 360 degrees, and butter and sugar a 4 x 8 loaf pan. 2. Add flour, baking powder, poppy seeds, and salt to a medium bowl
3. Add the sugar and lemon zest to a bowl, rub them together until sugar is yellow
4. Add the sugar mixture to a large mixing bowl and cream together with butter until light and smooth. Then, add the eggs, lemon extract, and vanilla extract to the bowl, mixing until a loose batter forms.
5. In a separate measuring glass, mix together the yogurt and milk.
6. While mixing, add 1/3 of the dry mixture and 1/2 of the yogurt mixture to the wet mixture in that order until all of the dry and yogurt mixture has been incorporated. (Start with dry and end with dry). Mix just until last flour streak has been incorporated. Do not overmix.
7. Pour batter into your prepped loaf pan and put in the oven for 45 minutes or until an inserted toothpick is not wet. Moist crumbs are fine, the loaf will finish setting in pan. Keep in mind all ovens are different and elevation may affect temperature or time required.
